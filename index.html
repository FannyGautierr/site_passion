    Qu'est ce que c'est ?

    Bo Burnham: Inside is a 2021 American special written, directed, filmed, edited, and performed by comedian Bo Burnham.[1] Recorded in the guest house of his Los Angeles home during the COVID-19 pandemic without a crew or audience, it was released on Netflix on May 30, 2021. Featuring a variety of songs and sketches about Burnham's day-to-day life indoors, it depicts his deteriorating mental health and explores themes of performativity and his relationship to the Internet and the audience it helped him reach, as well as addressing issues including climate change and social movements. Other segments discuss online activities such as calling one's mother on FaceTime, posting on Instagram, sexting, and video game streaming.
